Fun Facts About Bugs!

1)Houseflies find sugar with their feet, which are 10 million times more sensitive than human tongues.

2)Ticks can grow from the size of a grain of rice to the size of a marble.

3)Approximately 2,000 silkworm cocoons are needed to produce one pound of silk.

4)While gathering food, a bee may fly up to 60 miles in one day.

5)Ants can lift and carry more than fifty times their own weight.

6)It takes about one hundred Monarch Butterflies to weigh an ounce.

7)Honeybees have to make about ten million trips to collect enough nectar for production of one pound of honey.

8)Insects have been present for about 350 million years, and humans for only 300,000 years.

9)There are nearly as many species of ants (8,800) as there are species of birds (9,000) in the world.

10)The oldest known fossil of an insect dates back 400 million years and is a springtail.
